Civil engineering shapes our world's infrastructure, from bridges to water systems. They design, build, and maintain these structures, ensuring a safe and sustainable built environment.

Architect

Established in 2014, Rajiv Gandhi Government Engineering College (RGGEC)

is a government-funded engineering college located in Kangra, Himachal Pradesh. Affiliated with Himachal Pradesh Technical University and approved by AICTE, the college offers undergraduate programs in engineering disciplines. They currently have a four-year Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) program with a few specializations and a five-year Bachelor of Architecture (B.Arch) program. RGGEC also boasts a dedicated training and placement cell to assist students in securing job opportunities.
